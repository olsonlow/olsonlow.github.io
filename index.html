Welcome to Sprotocols

This iOS application was crated as a senior design project in 2014.

Authors:Luke Vergos, Zach Dahlgren, Lowell Olson

The idea for this app came from a cardiologist in Santa Rosa CA, who
wanted an easier way to share patient care and diagnostic/treatment capabilities
with general practicioners.

Sprotocols is the resulting attempt at this.
Sprotocols allows a specialist to dynamically create medical protocols within the app
and share them on the app. Other doctors with access to the app, in user mode
can then use the protocols in their patient care routines.

The app is not totally complete and requires some collaboration with doctors to
make some decisions about how the calculation funcitions should be completed.
However, it is in a state of completeness that allows it to be demonstrated.
